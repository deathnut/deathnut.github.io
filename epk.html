DEATHNUT

Hardcore Thrash from the PNW

Formed: 2022
Genre: Hardcore / Thrash / Metal
Label: Independent

Hailing from the Pacific Northwest, the NUT is loose and there's no going back. DEATHNUT unapologetically blends high energy thrash riffage with the raw intensity of hardcore breakdowns built to shake the room, all while not taking themselves too seriously.

For fans of: Power Trip, Spite, Pantera, Lamb of God
